BRIDGES(V3 OPEN)

As BRC20 and Ethereum are independent of each other, the assets on the chain cannot circulate directly for summary transactions. There are two main existing solutions, namely cross-chain atomic exchange and cross-chain atomic mapping.

Cross-chain atomic exchanges are executed through hash time-locked contracts, and the counterparties must complete the transaction and provide the transaction private key within the specified time. Although this kind of transaction is safe, it requires both parties to be online at the same time, and the confirmation time is long, which is not friendly enough for ordinary small transactions.

Cross-chain atomic mapping is to lock the assets of one chain and cast the same amount of assets to another chain in 1:1 at the same time, so as to realize the fast matching between the assets on the other chain. Taking BTC as an example, there are many issuers on Ethereum that have used this solution to cast ERC20 BTC, such as wBTC, tBTC, imBTC, hBTC, etc. CatFish will choose cross-chain atom mapping to complete the cross-chain asset mapping and form a complete cross-chain asset trading system for BRC20.
