WITH CATFISH. THE YIELD PROTOCOL

CatFish Vault is the first license-free multi-chain revenue automation protocol for the BRC20 ecosystem. Uses serial number protocol, Biellmann algorithm to earn revenue for BTC native BRC20 assets. Provides a on chain token revenue pool including BRC20 assets, EVM chain.

REVENUE MAXIMIZATION

CatFish Vault automates the liquidity management of funds through smart contracts and adjusts between liquidity pools. It dynamically allocates funds to the liquidity pools with the highest potential returns based on factors such as current market conditions, reward rates and risk indicators.

Participants in the vault can maximize their returns and achieve higher rates of return by continuously optimizing the allocation of funds.

BOOST YOUR REWARDS

Supply any supported BRC20 tokens to market vaults to earn variable interest and CatFish tokens ($CAFI) daily by providing any supported BRC20 token to the Market Vault, including $ORDI, $SATS, $RATS, and other leading BRC20 tokens. When you need to use your assets, you can withdraw them immediately.

In version 2, $CAFI holders can participate in the CatFish Stake (similar to the ERC20 Staking), where holders can put their $CAFI tokens in for an additional bonus.

INCENTIVES AND BUYBACKS

The vast majority of the revenue generated by the vault goes to the user. All commissions are used partly to repurchase and partly to reward $CAFI users for locking positions in DAO. The platform commission is partly used to buy back $CAFI.
